Frequently Asked Questions

What is Wander Wyze Explorer Club?

Wander Wyze Explorer Club is a yearly travel membership by Wander Wyze Holidays. Members get travel credits, member-only deals, priority support, early access to offers, and a chance to win sponsored holidays.

What is the membership price?

The founding membership price is ₹999 per year.

How long is the membership valid?

The membership is valid for 1 year from the date of joining / membership activation.

What is the Goa giveaway?

Once we reach our first 100 founding members, one lucky member will be selected for a sponsored Goa couple holiday for 2 people.

What is included in the Goa giveaway?

The tentative giveaway includes a 3 Nights / 4 Days Goa couple holiday with return flights, 4-star hotel stay, breakfast, and travel assistance by Wander Wyze Holidays. Final inclusions will depend on dates, availability, flights, hotel options, and final giveaway terms.

What if I don’t win the Goa holiday?

You will still receive a ₹1,000 Wander Wyze Travel Credit, which can be used on eligible future bookings through Wander Wyze Holidays.

How long is the ₹1,000 travel credit valid?

The ₹1,000 travel credit is valid for 12 months from membership activation.

Can I use the ₹1,000 credit on any booking?

The credit can be used on eligible hotel bookings of ₹20,000 or above, or eligible holiday package / flights + hotel bookings of ₹50,000 or above.

No. It is a travel credit and can only be used on eligible bookings through Wander Wyze Holidays. It is not redeemable for cash.

We will try to match it, beat it, or suggest a better-value option wherever available.

How will I get my membership ID?

Membership ID will be sent by email/WhatsApp within 24 hours after successful payment.

No. The membership fee is non-refundable once purchased.
