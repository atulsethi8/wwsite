Dubai Combos – FAQs

What’s the difference between Standard and Premium Desert Safari?

Premium includes better camp seating (often VIP/sofa), upgraded buffet, sometimes table service and slightly longer dune-bashing. We’ll share both options while quoting.

Can I swap Dhow Cruise for Dubai Frame or MOTF?

Yes, we customise. You can replace or add experiences based on time and budget.

Are transfers private?

Default is shared transfers for standard combos. Private transfers can be added to all items—recommended for comfort and speed.

What’s the best time for deals?

Summer has the sharpest deals; winter is peak with limited availability—book early.
